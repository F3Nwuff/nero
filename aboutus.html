About Us


We are a team of passionate health enthusiasts who believe in the power of staying hydrated. We understand that life can get busy and it can be easy to forget to drink enough water. That's why we created Nero, our hydration reminder app, designed to help you stay on top of your daily water intake.

Our app is simple to use, with a user-friendly interface that allows you to set personalized hydration goals and reminders. Whether you're at work, exercising, or just going about your day, our app will send you timely reminders to drink water, helping you stay on track with your hydration goals. Additionally, you may use our website here to track your statistics on your computer.

Our app also comes with a water tracking feature, which allows you to log your water intake and monitor your progress. This is a great way to see how much water you're consuming and make sure you're reaching your daily water goals.



Why Nero?


We are committed to making our app accessible to everyone, which is why we offer it for free on the App Store and Google Play. Our goal is to help as many people as possible stay hydrated and improve their overall health and well-being. In conclusion, our app is the perfect tool to help you stay on top of your hydration needs, making it easy to drink more water and maintain a healthy lifestyle. Download our app today and start reaping the benefits of staying hydrated.
